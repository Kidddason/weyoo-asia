Who We Are

Weyoo Asia is a supplier and distributor of science laboratory equipment, analytical instruments and weighing systems. We serve pharmaceutical, food and beverage, education, research, healthcare and industrial customers.

Our goal is to be a top-tier provider of quality instruments — pairing trusted global brands with the local expertise needed to install, calibrate and maintain them properly.

Every instrument we supply is backed by trained engineers, genuine spare parts and responsive after-sales service.
